About Me

Passionate about the intersection of AI, graphics, and quantum computing

Hi there! I'm Gyanateet, a passionate researcher and developer at the forefront of artificial intelligence and computer graphics. Currently pursuing my Master's degree in Computer Science & AI at the University of Leeds, I'm dedicated to exploring the fascinating intersection of machine learning, quantum computing, and visual technologies.

My journey in technology has led me through various exciting domains - from developing cutting-edge neural networks for image generation to researching quantum algorithms for optimization problems. I'm particularly fascinated by how we can leverage AI to create more immersive and intelligent digital experiences.

When I'm not diving deep into research papers or coding the next breakthrough algorithm, you'll find me exploring the latest advancements in GPU computing, experimenting with generative AI models, or contributing to open-source projects that push the boundaries of what's possible in computer graphics and machine learning.
